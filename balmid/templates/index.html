About BALMID industries

Balmid Industries Limited is a multi-producing company in which one of its primary purposes is expanding the agricultural sector of the nation. We are a group of company which focuses on the betterment and survival of the agricultural body and creating more employment opportunities for the youths and young adults. In 2016, the company was first launched under the name Horbal Nigeria Enterprise, which engaged in the manufacturing of food and drink products and Poultry farming. But as time goes on, we decided to expand our branches and focus on farming on larger scales with the help of providing constant and non- fluctuating of food supply to the nation at large.
